About

I build websites, but what I'm really building is trust between a business and its customers.

My path into frontend development started with a simple observation: most businesses lose customers before they ever pick up the phone, because their website doesn't reflect the quality of what they actually do. That gap between good work and a website that undersells it is what I spend my time closing.

Every project starts with the same question — what does this business need a visitor to believe in the first ten seconds? From there, design and code become tools in service of that answer: clear hierarchy, honest copy, fast load times, and interactions that feel considered rather than decorative. I'm not just writing HTML and Tailwind, I'm shaping how a business is perceived online.
